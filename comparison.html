Psychopath vs Sociopath

Trait Psychopath Sociopath
Emotional Response Shallow, cold, unemotional Volatile, angry, emotional
Behavior Calculated, manipulative Impulsive, erratic
Social Relationships Superficial charm, often no long-term bonds Some attachments, but unstable
Origin Likely genetic and brain differences Often due to environment and trauma
