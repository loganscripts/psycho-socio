Psychopath vs. Sociopath

Psychopath

  • Highly manipulative, often charming
  • No empathy or guilt
  • Usually planned and controlled behavior
  • Can maintain a normal life
  • Extremely dangerous in secret

Sociopath

  • Impulsive and erratic behavior
  • May feel guilt or remorse occasionally
  • More emotional outbursts
  • Difficulty forming long-term relationships
  • Often caught due to reckless actions
