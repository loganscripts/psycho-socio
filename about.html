About MindMate

MindMate is your friendly, calm, and clever chatbot assistant designed to help you learn about the differences between psychopaths and sociopaths in a clear, approachable way. It supports English and Russian languages, voice narration, and a sleek, dark-themed interface to make your experience smooth and enjoyable.

Developed with accessibility and user comfort in mind, MindMate offers interactive features including quizzes, voice narration toggles, and a floating chatbot panel for instant help and guidance.
